How this cost is computed honesty

Premium is a fixed $10K/mo bill (2 × P1). LP allocates it by each item's share of consumed CU that day (cu_share × monthly_cost), recomputed daily and volume-weighted over the window. It is not consumption-priced — utilization over 100% (see Capacity) does not raise the bill. Capacity-SKU-level cost is a ✗ gap until the capacity-identity bridge lands (candidate 1); workspace-level (above) works today.
Spend is fully attributable and well-utilized. The actionable $ is the $1.25K/mo of orphan-suspect dataflows — gated behind lineage, not a quick retire.
