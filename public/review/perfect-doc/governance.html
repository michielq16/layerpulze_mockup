PII exposure surface

The DimEmployee and FactCompensation tables are joined to the Finance Global Model but only exposed through three RLS-protected reports: HR Cost Allocation (audience: HR managers), Compensation Review Quarterly (audience: VP+ leadership), and Audit Drilldown (audience: Internal Audit). LayerPulse's RLS evaluation confirms no breach exposure — the data is properly walled. The gap is the missing classification label, which is a documentation/audit-trail issue, not a data-exposure one.

Why this is the #1 risk

Under ISO 27001 A.8.2 and GDPR Article 30, sensitive data without explicit classification is treated as undeclared — and undeclared sensitive data joined to a broadly-shared model triggers an audit finding regardless of actual exposure. The fix is 14 days of classification work; the consequence of not fixing is a probable Major-tier audit finding.
