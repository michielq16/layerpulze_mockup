The one-sentence version

What we're building

Fabric knows your column types and DAX. It doesn't know what "NRR" means in your business, who decides that, or where the policy lives.

The Business Glossary is one tenant-wide dictionary your team owns. Define a term once, attach it to the models that use it, and that definition shows up everywhere — including auto-generated documents.

What's in a term

Six kinds of term

Every entry has a definition, an owner, an expert (SME), a status, and a sensitivity level. Each is one of six types:

Metric KPI Dimension Business term Acronym Process
The payoff

Attach a term → it flows everywhere

Define AOV once→ Attach to the [AOV] measure→ Shows on the model→ Becomes the measure's description in every document

If a measure has no attached term, its description stays blank on purpose — that empty space is the nudge to add one. No invented descriptions.

Two ways to read it

A–Z or cards

A–Z (default)

Reads like a real dictionary — jump to a letter, scan term + type + one-line definition. Best for browsing and onboarding.

Cards

A visual grid you can sort and filter by type, domain, status, owner. Best for governance + cleanup.
